Vision, Motto and Mission

Our Vision

  • In pursuit of excellence in education among the youth.
  • In making the student a complete citizen of the Nation.
  • To Inculcate a scientific temper.
  • To instill a passion for learning

Our Motto

  • Ask Right Questions – Be a Sardar

Our Mission

  • The empowerment of the Socially, Economically and Educationally marginalized sections of the society in Telangana Region and in particular, Rural Telangana. To sensitize students towards gender equality.

Life at Sardar Patel College

  • Admission &Fee Committee : Screening applications of allotted candidates by DOST. Guiding students to select the second languages. Fee committee helps students and parents in understanding the fee structure
  • Academic & Examination Committee : Academic committee helps students to select the SEC courses. Examination committee undertakes Internal Examinations.
  • Internal Quality Assurance Committee : Quality Assurance of the college is looked upon by IQAC.
  • Time Table Committee : Time table committee frames the Time Table for the whole college.
  • Library Advisory Committee : This committee takes cares off the library books and journals. It is mostly funded by UGC.
